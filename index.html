One terrifying night, I was behind the wheel, my trainer asleep in the sleeper cab behind me. The exhaustion was overwhelming, and suddenly, I experienced a micro-sleep. Just for a few seconds, my brain shut down, no matter how much coffee I‚Äôd tried to drink. We didn't die that night, but the sheer terror of what almost occurred has never left me.

Why didn't I get sleep while the truck was being loaded? Because of a tragic incident at a Walmart, where a forklift loading a truck crashed through the sleeper cab and killed someone.
