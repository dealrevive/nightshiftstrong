My Journey: From Wake-Up Call to Coaching Mission

My Story: The Wake-Up Call

Years ago, I was a new CDL driver, just like many of you. The relentless night shifts and constant fatigue eventually caught up to me.

One terrifying night, while driving a truck, I experienced a micro-sleep. I was seconds away from a catastrophic disaster. I'm sure my trainer, who was sleeping in the back, would have died! Out of nowhere, another trucker honked and flashed their lights, jolting me awake. Nothing happened, but it was a horrifying reminder of how dangerous sleep deprivation can be – not just for us, but for everyone on the road.

That moment changed everything. It ignited my passion to help others avoid what I almost faced. My mission is clear: to ensure no one else faces the dangers of night shift fatigue, and that every night shift worker can live a full, healthy, and strong life.
