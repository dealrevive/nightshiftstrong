The Night Shift Challenge: You're Not Alone

Most night shift workers, especially truckers, nurses, and security staff, never get real, sustainable help for the unique challenges they face. You're constantly battling fatigue, struggling to sleep during the day, relying on endless caffeine, and feeling disconnected. You're tired, wired, and frustrated – and it impacts everything from your safety on the road to your relationships at home.

I've been there too. I understand the silent struggle of the night shift because I've lived it. And I've coached thousands through recovery, performance, and lifestyle resets that actually work for *your* schedule.

My Wake-Up Call: Why I Coach Night Shift Strong

Years ago, I was a new CDL driver, just like many of you. The relentless night shifts, the constant fatigue – it all caught up to me. One night, while driving a truck, I experienced a micro-sleep. I was seconds away from a catastrophic disaster. Out of nowhere, another trucker honked and flashed their lights, jolting me awake. Nothing happened that night, but it was a terrifying reminder of how dangerous sleep deprivation can be, not just for us, but for everyone on the road.

That moment changed everything. It ignited my passion to help others avoid what I almost faced. My background as an addiction counselor at IOU University, where I provided group coaching for thousands and one-on-one sessions for over 15 clients, has given me deep insights into behavior change and personal transformation. While I'm currently expanding my expertise in sleep, stress, and recovery coaching through the Precision Nutrition program, my foundation in guiding individuals through profound life improvements is extensive.

I'm even rejoining a trucker training program (like Swift) to stay immersed in the daily realities of night shift work, ensuring my coaching remains relevant and grounded. My commitment extends beyond theory: I've been personally hired by a family to live with them and coach the husband in improving all aspects of his life – mental, physical, career, marriage, and anger management. This hands-on experience underscores my dedication to holistic, real-world transformation. My mission is to ensure no one else faces the dangers of night shift fatigue, and that every night shift worker can live a full, healthy, and strong life.
